Our Story

About Livin Cafe

Where mindful dining meets the best view in Tbilisi

Perched atop the Livin Hotel at 44 Samreklo Street in Old Tbilisi's Avlabari district, we offer the best 360° rooftop view of the city and Holy Trinity Cathedral. Everything on our menu is purely vegetarian and vegan вЂ” made fresh every day from plant-based ingredients with no compromise on taste.

"We believe great food doesn't need to come at the cost of the planet."
